My Travels

OMG, Israel was absolutely life-changing! 🇮🇱✨ From floating in the Dead Sea (best natural spa day ever!) to dancing the night away in Tel Aviv's incredible beach clubs, every moment was pure magic. The food scene totally blew my mind - I'm still dreaming about those fresh falafels and the most amazing hummus I've ever tasted! Jerusalem was such a vibe, walking through those ancient stone streets felt like stepping back in time, but with the coolest modern twist. Made some amazing friends at my hostel in the Old City, and we spent nights on the rooftop watching the sunset over the city - literally breathtaking! Had the most incredible Shabbat dinner with a local family who treated me like their own daughter. Also went on this amazing hike in the Negev Desert at sunrise - definitely worth the 4 AM wake-up call! The markets (they call them shuks) were absolutely insane, full of colors, spices, and the most amazing energy. Israel totally stole my heart and I'm already planning my next trip back! 💕
Living my best Italian life was everything I dreamed of and more! 🇮🇹 Started in Rome where I tossed a coin in the Trevi Fountain (definitely coming back!), and literally cried when I saw the Sistine Chapel - it's THAT beautiful! Venice was like stepping into a fairytale, even got lost in the tiny streets which led to finding this adorable family-run pasta place. The owner taught me how to properly twirl spaghetti! Florence completely stole my heart with its art and architecture - spent hours in the Uffizi Gallery and probably took a thousand photos of the Duomo. Don't even get me started on the gelato - tried a new flavor every single day (pistachio is the winner!). Took a cooking class in Tuscany where we made pasta from scratch in this gorgeous villa overlooking the countryside. The Amalfi Coast was straight out of a postcard - those colorful buildings, crystal clear water, and the freshest limoncello! Made friends with other travelers and we spent evenings sharing wine and stories on our hostel's terrace. Italy isn't just a place, it's a feeling! ✨🍝
Paris wasn't just a city, it was a whole mood! 🇫🇷 Started every morning with fresh croissants and café au lait at the cutest little sidewalk cafés. The Eiffel Tower at night is even more magical than in photos - literally squealed when I saw it sparkle for the first time! Spent hours getting lost in the Louvre (Mona Lisa is smaller than you'd think!), and discovered the most amazing vintage shops in Le Marais. Found this hidden jazz bar in the Latin Quarter where I spent the evening feeling like I was in Midnight in Paris! The French Riviera was an absolute dream - spent days beach-hopping in Nice and living that luxury life in Monaco (even if just for a day!). Took a lavender field tour in Provence that was literally what Instagram dreams are made of! The locals weren't rude at all - they loved it when I tried speaking French, even though I probably sounded ridiculous. Made besties with my roommates at the hostel, and we had the most amazing picnic by the Seine with baguettes, cheese, and wine. Pure bliss! ✨🥖
Spain was seriously the most incredible adventure! 🇪🇸 Barcelona completely stole my heart - Gaudi's architecture is like something from another planet! Park Güell at sunset was literally breathtaking, and the Sagrada Familia left me speechless. Learned to make authentic paella in this amazing cooking class, and discovered that sangria tastes SO much better in Spain! Madrid's energy was unreal - spent nights hopping between tapas bars in La Latina (discovered my obsession with patatas bravas!), and danced until sunrise at the coolest rooftop bars. The locals taught me the real way to eat churros (apparently you're supposed to dip them in hot chocolate!). Fell in love with siesta culture - afternoon naps should definitely be a thing everywhere! Took a spontaneous trip to Seville where I watched the most passionate flamenco show and wandered through the stunning Real Alcázar. The Spanish lifestyle is just different - dinner at 10 PM, dancing until dawn, and living life to the fullest. Found my soul city here! ✨💃
Greece was literally a dream come true! 🇬🇷 Started in Athens where I channeled my inner goddess at the Acropolis - the history here is absolutely mind-blowing! But the islands... OMG the islands! Santorini's sunsets were even more incredible than all the Instagram posts (if that's even possible!). Spent days exploring the white-washed streets of Oia, finding the cutest photo spots, and eating the most amazing Greek salads (the feta cheese here is life-changing!). Island-hopped to Mykonos where I danced on beach bars with people from all over the world - the energy was unreal! Made the best friends at my hostel and we spent nights sharing stories over Greek wine and souvlaki. Discovered hidden beaches in Milos that looked like they were from another planet! The locals were the sweetest - this adorable yiayia (grandmother) taught me how to make proper tzatziki! Also tried cliff jumping for the first time (terrifying but so worth it for the pics!). Greece isn't just beautiful, it's pure magic! ✨🏺
London literally stole my heart! 🇬🇧 Started my mornings with proper English breakfast (beans on toast is actually amazing?!) and spent afternoons exploring the coolest neighborhoods. Camden Market was a total vibe - vintage shopping, street food from around the world, and the most amazing atmosphere! Got lost in the British Museum for hours, and yes, I did the touristy photos at Platform 9¾ (total Harry Potter nerd moment!). Found the cutest hidden bookshops in Notting Hill, and spent rainy afternoons in the coziest cafes. The pub culture is so fun - made friends with locals who taught me proper British slang! Took day trips to Bath and Cambridge where I tried punting (harder than it looks!) and felt like I was in a Jane Austen novel. Afternoon tea at Sketch was literally a pink paradise, and the views from Sky Garden at sunset were absolutely incredible! Even the gloomy weather couldn't dampen my mood - there's just something so magical about London's energy. Definitely left a piece of my heart here! ✨👑
